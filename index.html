Imagine a cozy houseboat gently swaying on Amsterdam’s iconic canals. Now, picture that same houseboat filled with adorable cats who’ve been given a second chance at life. Since 1968, The Catboat has been rescuing and caring for stray and abandoned cats, all while keeping their paws dry in the heart of the city

Water and cats don’t usually mix, right? But the clever folks at The Catboat have mastered this pairing. Here, cats learn to love life on the water, surrounded by caring humans and the gentle ebb and flow of Amsterdam’s waterways.
