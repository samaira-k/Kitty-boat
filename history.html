History

1966

It all began with one sweet mama cat and her kittens, who found a cozy new home with kind Mrs. v. Weelde.

1968

With more furry friends arriving, the clever idea to create a floating kitty haven on an old sailing barge came to life.

1971

The cats outgrew their first boat, so a second one joined the family, and soon visitors came to adopt or just admire the unique "catboat".

1987

The Catboat officially became Stichting de Poezenboot, a purr-fectly charming charity for cats in need.

2001 & beyond

One of the boats got a big makeover, and its first happy resident, Bloemetje ("Little Flower"), moved in on 3 April 2002.
